Lentil Soup

Comforting, healthy, and budget-friendly — a perfect one-pot meal.

Skill Level: Easy

Estimated Cost: ~$2.00/serving

Ingredients

  • 1 cup dried lentils, rinsed
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 2 carrots, diced
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 4 cups vegetable broth or water
  • 1 tsp cumin
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Oil for cooking

Instructions

  1. In a pot, heat oil, then sauté onions, garlic, and carrots for 5 minutes.
  2. Add lentils, broth, cumin, salt, and pepper.
  3. Bring to a boil, then reduce heat and simmer for 25–30 minutes.
  4. Blend slightly if desired. Serve warm with bread or rice.
