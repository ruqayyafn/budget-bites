Budget Pasta

A simple, affordable pasta dish made with minimal ingredients — perfect for students or families on a budget.

Skill Level: Easy

Estimated Cost: ~$2.50/serving

Ingredients

  • 200g pasta (any type)
  • 2 tbsp olive oil
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 can diced tomatoes
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Optional: grated cheese or herbs

Instructions

  1. Boil pasta according to package instructions. Drain and set aside.
  2. Heat olive oil in a pan and sauté garlic until golden.
  3. Add canned tomatoes, salt, and pepper. Simmer for 10 minutes.
  4. Mix in the pasta and stir well until coated with sauce.
  5. Serve hot with optional cheese or herbs on top.
