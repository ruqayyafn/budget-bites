Chickpea Curry

A hearty, plant-based curry that’s full of flavor and light on the wallet.

Skill Level: Medium

Estimated Cost: ~$3.00/serving

Ingredients

  • 1 can chickpeas, drained
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 tbsp curry powder
  • 1 can diced tomatoes
  • 1/2 cup coconut milk (optional)
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Oil for cooking

Instructions

  1. Heat oil in a pot and sauté onions and garlic until soft.
  2. Add curry powder and cook for 1 minute to release flavor.
  3. Stir in chickpeas and tomatoes. Add coconut milk if using.
  4. Simmer uncovered for 15–20 minutes, stirring occasionally.
  5. Serve over rice or with flatbread.
