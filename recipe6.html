Egg Fried Rice

A quick, flavorful meal perfect for using leftover rice and simple ingredients.

Skill Level: Easy

Estimated Cost: ~$1.50/serving

Ingredients

  • 2 cups cooked rice (preferably day-old)
  • 2 eggs, beaten
  • 1/2 cup mixed vegetables (carrots, peas, corn)
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 2 tbsp soy sauce
  • 2 tbsp oil
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Optional: green onions for garnish

Instructions

  1. Heat oil in a pan or wok over medium heat.
  2. Scramble the eggs until cooked, then set aside.
  3. Saut√© garlic and mixed vegetables until tender.
  4. Add cooked rice and soy sauce, stirring well to combine.
  5. Return eggs to the pan and mix thoroughly.
  6. Season with salt and pepper, garnish with green onions, and serve hot.
