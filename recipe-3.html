Simple Omelette

A quick and protein-packed meal using just a few kitchen staples.

Skill Level: Easy

Estimated Cost: ~$1.00/serving

Ingredients

  • 2 eggs
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 1 tbsp milk (optional)
  • 1 tbsp oil or butter
  • Optional fillings: cheese, herbs, veggies

Instructions

  1. Beat eggs with milk (if using), salt, and pepper in a bowl.
  2. Heat oil or butter in a nonstick pan over medium heat.
  3. Pour in the egg mixture and tilt pan to spread evenly.
  4. Cook for 2–3 minutes until edges firm up. Add any fillings.
  5. Fold and cook for 1–2 more minutes. Serve hot.
