Fresh Caesar Salad

A light and refreshing salad thatâ€™s quick to throw together on a tight budget.

Skill Level: Easy

Estimated Cost: ~$2.50/serving

Ingredients

  • 1 romaine lettuce head, chopped
  • Croutons
  • Parmesan cheese
  • Caesar dressing (store-bought or homemade)
  • Optional: grilled chicken or boiled egg

Instructions

  1. Wash and dry the lettuce.
  2. Toss lettuce with dressing in a large bowl.
  3. Add croutons and Parmesan. Mix gently.
  4. Top with optional protein. Serve chilled.
